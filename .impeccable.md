# .impeccable.md — ESG Value Engine Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Accenture judges (senior consultants, GSIC 2026 competition). They evaluate business impact,
technical credibility, and presentation quality in a short demo window. They are sophisticated
— they will notice lazy design and reward precise, confident execution.

### Brand Personality
**Innovative · Authoritative · Precise**

Voice: speaks with the confidence of a Bloomberg terminal and the polish of a Series B SaaS
product. No decoration for decoration's sake — every element justifies its existence.

Emotional goal: the first 5 seconds should make the judge think "this team built something real."

### Aesthetic Direction
**Reference: Palantir Gotham** — intelligence-grade dark UI.
- Serious, powerful, slightly intimidating
- Data-first: numbers are heroes, text is supporting cast
- Monospace for all data values and financial figures
- Thin 1px dividers, precise grid alignment
- Subdued palette with surgical accent color use
- Subtle background texture (dot grid or scan lines) for depth
- High contrast: pure white for critical data, #787878 for context

Anti-references:
- NOT generic SaaS purple gradient (too startup-y)
- NOT playful / rounded / consumer-app aesthetics
- NOT information overload without hierarchy

### Design Principles
1. **Numbers are heroes** — financial figures, scores, and IRR values should be the largest,
   most prominent elements on screen. Use GT Sectra Fine or monospace for impact.
2. **Earn every pixel** — no decorative elements. Borders, dividers, and spacing exist to
   separate signal from noise, not to look pretty.
3. **Surgical color use** — #AC00EF and #F04FDB are scalpels, not paintbrushes. Reserve them
   for the single most important piece of information in each section.
4. **Motion signals intelligence** — animations should feel computed, not designed. Stagger
   reveals like data loading from an API. No bounces, no spring physics.
5. **Hierarchy through weight and size, not color** — three distinct text levels:
   display (GT Sectra Fine, large), label (Graphik 700, small caps), body (Graphik 400, #787878).
